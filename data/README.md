# 📦 data/ — Local Sample Data (never committed)

A place for **local sample data** on your own machine, used while developing.

**Owner:** Everyone (locally) — nothing here is shared
**Status:** 🔴 Git‑ignored — this folder's contents are **never** committed

> 🚫 **STOP — read this before adding any file.**
> This folder is **git‑ignored on purpose**. Real BRERC data is **confidential**.
> It can include **precise species locations** (which could put protected wildlife
> at risk) and **personal data** (like recorder names). None of it may ever enter
> git or GitHub — once something is pushed, it is very hard to fully remove.

## What goes here

- Small **sample** or **test** data on your local machine only.

## What does **not** go here

- ❌ **Real BRERC data** — never place it in the repo, even locally, if it could be
  committed by accident.
- ❌ **Anything sensitive** — precise locations of protected species, personal
  data, or anything BRERC has not agreed to make public.
- ❌ Anything you want your teammates to see — files here are **not** shared. To
  share data safely, agree the method with the team and BRERC first.

**If in doubt, do not add it.** Ask the team lead before working with real data.
